What is Augmented Reality in Real Estate business:

Augmented reality and sophisticated visualization are used in real estate to portray real-world environments, such as flats. In other words, the usage of augmented reality (AR) allows potential real estate purchasers to view the property in 3D. This method saves time and money for both real estate agents and potential buyers.

Benefits of AR Real Estate (Buyer’s Perspective)

  1. The buyer launches the augmented reality app and focuses it on a newspaper or printed advertisement.
  2. The programme captures the ad and transforms it into a fictitious 3D architectural model of the property..
  3. The buyer will have access to this model and will be able to move around it in order to receive a scenic picture of the space and observe it from all aspects.
  4. Realtors get global reach to anyone living in any part of the world.
  5. Helps to facilitate emotional links with the augmented reality tour that comes to life

Perfect way to sell property to picky customers

  1. 3D views of buildings and amenities with AR, including all landscape, motion, color, and textures, rendered in real time.
  2. Floor layouts in 3D with Augmented Reality
  3. Using a map to show the location of a property
  4. Floor plans, pictures, and other information can be found here.
